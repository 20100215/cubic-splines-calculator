Cubic Splines

Intro
  • Degree of 2 and more will result to a more complex curve as the degree increases
  • Splines are a line from one point to the next
  • If n is the number of points, there will always be n-1 splines
  • Degree of 3 since we are dealing with cubic polynomials

4 unknown coefficients:

  • a, b, c, d
  • each cubic spline will have 4 unknowns
  • each unknown coefficient has its own equation
